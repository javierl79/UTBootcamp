The purpose of this project was to analyze how weather changes as you get closer to the equator.

This graph shows the relationship between the latitude and tempreature. In this graph we can see that the closer we get to the equator (0) the tempreature (F) starts to rise.

Latitude vs Hummidity

This graph shows the relationship between the latitude and humidity. In this graph we can see that at the north pole and south pole there's no humidity.

Latitude vs Cloudiness

Latitude vs Wind Speed
